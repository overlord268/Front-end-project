... Our Pillars ...

SPARK EDEN'S PHILOSOPHY UNDERSTANDING

EdenSparks seeks to share the principles of fractal democracy with communities around the world. In Epoch #1 the focus will be on the LATAM EOS community with OpenMics, Zoom Calls, and Challenges based on Dan Larimer’s “More Equal Animals” book.

SPARK CODING DEVELOPMENT SKILLS

EOS Blockchain opens a world of new opportunities. To execute this vision, a new generation of coders needs to be supported and managed. EdenSpark will support grants for the development of key DAC members to build up their skills by learning AGILE project management (PMP), Design, and Programming.

SPARK GLOBAL ECONOMY

EdenSparks will create environments to meet and discuss ideas and business opportunities across different regions of the world. Through effective peer coordination, we want to create an EOS-based marketplace for freelancers to offer simple services and for service seekers to find trustworthy freelancers.

SPARK COMMUNITY CREATIVITY

EdenSparks will support gamified ways for NFT adoption using ART and MUSIC to spark the imagination of the community.
